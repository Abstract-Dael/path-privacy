Privacy Policy - Path

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

At Path, we believe your personal information belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account using Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Your unique user ID
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Display name (if provided)

Goal Data: To provide our goal tracking functionality, we store:

  • Goal titles and descriptions
  • Target numbers and units
  • Your personal reasons/meanings for each goal
  • Progress entries (dates, amounts, mood selections, and notes)
  • Start dates and completion status

Remember Your Why (RYW) Media: When you choose to upload photos or videos as motivational anchors:

  • Images and videos you select from your device
  • These files are stored securely and are only accessible by you
  • We do not scan, analyze, or use this media for any purpose other than displaying it to you

Usage Data: We collect basic analytics to improve the app:

  • App opens and session duration
  • Feature usage (which screens you visit)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Path app functionality
  • Sync your goals and progress across your devices
  • Display your "Remember Your Why" media back to you
  • Send you notifications about your goals (only if you enable them)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when you contact us

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your goals or progress data for advertising
  • Share your data with other users
  • Analyze the content of your photos or videos
  • Train AI models on your personal data

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), which provides:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Industry-standard data protection measures

Your "Remember Your Why" photos and videos are stored in Firebase Storage with restricted access - only you can view your own media.

Third-Party Services

Path uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and file storage
  • Apple Sign-In: Secure authentication

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Export: You can view all your data within the app at any time.

Deletion: You can delete individual goals, progress entries, or media files at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, use the "Delete Account" option in Settings.

Account Disconnection: You can disconnect your Apple or Google account from Settings.

Notifications: You can disable notifications through your device settings.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal information within 30 days, including:

  • All goal data and progress entries
  • All uploaded photos and videos
  • Your account information

Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement purposes.

Children's Privacy

Path is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Path is available worldwide. Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where Firebase operates. By using Path, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of Path after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
